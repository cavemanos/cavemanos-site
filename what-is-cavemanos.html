What is CavemanOS?

CavemanOS is a local-first system for building durable tools, workflows, and intelligent interfaces.

It is currently in development and focuses on clarity, control, and software that remains understandable as it grows more capable.

The project is built around a simple belief: powerful systems should stay inspectable, durable, and owned by the person using them.
